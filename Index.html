Frequently Asked Questions

What is an Invisible Character?

An invisible character is a special Unicode character that looks empty but can still be copied and pasted into supported games and apps.

Does this work in Free Fire?

Yes. Many Free Fire versions support invisible Unicode characters, although support may change after game updates.

Are Fancy Fonts safe?

Yes. These fonts use Unicode characters and are safe to copy and paste where Unicode is supported.
